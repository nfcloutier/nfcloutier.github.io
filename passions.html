Freedom Dance Foundation
and the Freedom School of Dance

The Freedom School of Dance is an Edmonton-based children's dance studio aimed at making dance accessible to all children, regardless of their socio-economic background. Their goal is to provide all students with the assistance they need, not only in their dancing but in their self-confidence. On top of teaching proper dance techniques, FSD encourages every child's confidence, creativity, potential and spirit!

For more info check out their website.

The Great Outdoors

Despite my grand stature, I do enjoy working up a sweat on my way to an isolated locale with a majestic view. My personal highlights include my time in the Canadian Rockies, in the Grand Canyon and in the Fjordland National Park of New Zealand.

Mental Health

As a person who has suffered the tragic and premature loss of cherished friends and family and who has had some experience with the intimidating world of the mental healthcare system, I would be remiss if I did not implore others to take their mental health seriously and seek assistance if they are struggling. Choosing to embark on the path of progress can be difficult and taking the first step is often daunting, but I promise it is worth it!
